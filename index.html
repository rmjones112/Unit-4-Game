Below you'll find 4 crystals and by clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to score to win, you lose the game if your total score goes above the score to win.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

The crystal's values reset every game. Good Luck!
